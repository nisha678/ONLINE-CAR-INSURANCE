How to Claim Insurance

               1. Call your insurance company immediately after the accident and inform about 
                   the damage.
               2. Intimate the police about the incident and obtain an FIR.
               3. Record the details of the car, the driver, and the witnesses in the FIR.
               4. File a claim with your insurance company and ask them to assign a surveyor to 
                   evaluate the loss.
               5. You can submit the claim online as well, provided that your insurer provides 
                   this facility.
